PAIN OF THE HEART

PAIN OF THE HEART, a book by Yusuf Mafu Jerome, is a Christian motivational book. It teaches us lessons about common mistakes we make in life that affect our God-given destiny, thereby giving us emotional pains, refered to here, as the "pain of the heart". This emotional trauma normally affect our spiritual and physical lives. The author is using his own life experience as a lesson for all to learn from his mistakes.
